Description: Note the lid of this yellow gas-pak jar outfitted with a catalyst screen, and 2 beakers labeled “New” and “Used” catalyst.

An anaerobic gas generator system such as the one depicted here is used for the rapid generation of an anaerobic environment, which is necessary when cultivating anaerobic microorganisms.
